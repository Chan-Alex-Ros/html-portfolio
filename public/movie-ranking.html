My Favorite Movies of All Time

Top 3 Movies

John Wick

A revenge movie about an ex hitman who was wanted to retire but came out of retirement to seek revenge for his dog. This movie was great because of the amazing action scenes and its overpowered main protagonist.

A silent voice

An anime about a deaf girl who was bullied in middle school. The bully grown up to regret his decision and fate brought them back together. Amazing soundtrack and teardropping emotional scene between the two main characters.

Spiderman

A guy got bitten by a radioactive spider which cause him to have super natural power of web slinging and spider sense. Every childhood movie and awesome web slinging scene that was revolutionary for a movie at that time.
